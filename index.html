Our Story

A Cozy Corner for Everyone

Tucked inside Zenex Plaza on New Road, Berry Lounge & Bar is Pokhara's warmest gathering spot — where golden lighting, soulful music, and exceptional food & drinks weave together into an evening you won't forget.

Whether you're raising a glass with old friends, on a romantic date, or simply unwinding after the mountains — Berry is your home away from home. A place full of good vibes and coziness, welcoming guests of all ages.
